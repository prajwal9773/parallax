Generative AI Startups

Deep Mind

DeepMind is a division of Alphabet, Inc. responsible for developing general-purpose artificial intelligence (AGI) technology. That technology is also known as Google DeepMind. DeepMind uses raw pixel data as input and learns from experience. The AI uses deep learning on a convolutional neural network, with a model-free reinforcement learning technique called Q-learning.

inflection AI

Inflection operates as an Artificial Intelligence(AI) studio which develops human-computer interaction. It enables users to speak to a computer program called Pi in a conversational manner. The company was founded in 2022 and is based in Palo Alto, California.

Cohere is a Canadian startup that provides natural language processing models that help companies improve human-machine interactions. Cohere was founded in 2019 by Aidan Gomez, Ivan Zhang, and Nick Frosst.
